Mission Statement


At Laura’s Dog Training, our mission is to promote the overall well-being of each dog we
work with by fostering a balanced, positive training experience. We believe that a dog’s
mental, emotional, and physical health are all interconnected, and our training programs
are designed to nurture each aspect of their development. Through compassionate,
science-based methods, we strive to help dogs become confident, well-behaved, and
happy, while strengthening the bond between them and their owners. Our commitment is
to create a harmonious environment where every dog feels safe, supported, and
empowered to thrive.

About Us


At Laura’s Dog Training, we believe that every dog is capable of learning, growing, and
thriving with the right guidance. Founded by Laura, a passionate and certified dog trainer,
our mission is to help dogs and their owners build strong, lasting relationships through
positive, effective training. With years of experience and a love for all things canine, Laura
tailors each training program to suit the unique needs of your dog, ensuring a personalized
approach that’s fun, supportive, and results-driven.
Laura’s gentle yet firm training methods focus on creating an environment of trust and
respect, where dogs feel empowered to learn and owners gain confidence in their abilities.
Whether you're looking to address behavioral challenges, teach new tricks, or simply
improve communication, we're here to help you every step of the way.
At the heart of our approach is the belief that a well-trained dog is a happy dog – and
a happy dog means a happy home.
Let’s work together to create a positive, harmonious relationship with your furry friend!

Behind the Leash


Laura is a certified professional dog trainer with extensive training in both canine behavior
and positive reinforcement techniques. She holds certifications from respected organizations like the
International Association of Canine Professionals (IACP) and Certification Council for Professional Dog Trainers (CCPDT),
ensuring that her methods are grounded in science and best practices. With ongoing education
and a passion for staying current with the latest training techniques, Laura is committed to providing the
highest level of expertise to help you and your dog succeed.
