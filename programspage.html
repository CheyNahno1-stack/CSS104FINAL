Furry Foundation

Starting with the basics? Laura’s Beginner Dog Training Program is the perfect way to set
your dog up for success. In this program, we focus on foundational skills like sit, stay,
recall, and leash walking, all while building a strong bond of trust and respect. Laura will
guide you through positive reinforcement techniques that are both effective and fun,
ensuring your dog learns essential manners and commands in a supportive, stress-free
environment. Whether you’re a first-time dog owner or just want to reinforce good habits,
this program is designed to give your pup the best start!

Tail Wagging Mastery

Ready to take your dog’s training to the next level? Laura’s Intermediate Dog Training
Program is designed for dogs who have mastered the basics and are ready to learn more
advanced skills. This program focuses on enhancing obedience, improving impulse
control, and addressing more complex behaviors like off-leash recall, better leash
manners, and handling distractions. Through fun and engaging exercises, Laura will help
your dog build confidence and sharpen their skills while continuing to reinforce a positive,
trusting relationship. Perfect for dogs who are ready to grow and thrive!



Alpha Paws

For dogs who are ready to master the art of obedience, Laura’s Advanced Dog Training
Program is the ultimate challenge. This program is designed to refine your dog’s skills,
tackle complex behaviors, and fine-tune off-leash control in various environments. With a
focus on advanced commands, problem-solving, and handling high-level distractions,
Laura will guide you and your dog through expert-level training that builds on everything
you've already accomplished. It’s time to push boundaries, strengthen communication,
and show off your dog’s impressive abilities!



Behavioral Transformation

Every dog is unique, and so is every training journey! Laura’s Customized Dog Training
Program allows you to choose exactly what you'd like to focus on or get personalized
recommendations based on your dog’s needs. Whether you're working on socialization,
leash reactivity, separation anxiety, or perfecting specific skills like recall or trick training,
Laura will create a tailored plan that’s just right for you and your dog. Not sure where to
start? No problem! Laura will assess your dog’s behavior and recommend the perfect
training approach to address any challenges and help you both succeed. Let’s create a
training plan that’s as unique as your pup!



Agility Adventures

Agility training with Laura is a barking-good way to bond with your pup, boost their
confidence, and unleash their paw-tential. From leaping over hurdles to weaving through
poles and zooming through tunnels, Laura’s paws-on guidance will have your dog racing to
the paw-dium in no time. Not only is agility training a tail-wagging adventure, but it also
keeps your furry friend mentally sharp and physically active—no couch paw-tatoes here!
With treats as motivation and Laura’s expert tips, your pup will master the course while
building trust and patience on the pause table. Start small, stay consistent, and watch as
practice makes paw-fect. Laura’s positive approach ensures you and your dog will have fun
every step of the way, zig-zags included. Whether it’s raising the woof on your dog’s skills or
sneaking in extra bonding time, this experience is pawsitively rewarding for both ends of the
leash. So grab your pup and get ready to make tails wag—Laura’s agility training is truly a
fur-tastic journey!



From Growls to Grins

Does your dog struggle with aggressive behavior? Laura’s compassionate and
effective aggression training helps transform growls into grins, one paw at a time.
Through positive reinforcement and personalized strategies, Laura addresses
triggers like fear, frustration, or territorial instincts, creating a safe and structured
environment for your pup to learn and thrive. By focusing on trust-building and
consistent guidance, you’ll see your dog grow more confident and calm in
challenging situations. With techniques tailored to your dog’s unique needs, Laura
equips you with the tools to handle tense moments and nurture better behavior
over time. Aggression training isn’t just about reducing reactive moments—it’s
about strengthening the bond between you and your furry friend while giving
them the confidence to navigate the world with ease. Together, you and Laura can
help your dog become the goodest boy or girl they’re meant to be!
